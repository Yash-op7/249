## 1. **Reversion Setup (VPVR)**

### **VAL / VAH Rejections**

When price attempts to leave value but fails.

**Use case:** Range day, low-volatility day, mean-reversion day.

**Entry Example:**

- Price tags VAH
    
- Wicks or shows rejection on LTF (1–5 min)
    
- Short back to POC or VAL

## 2. **Break & Acceptance – Trend Day**

A true breakout requires **acceptance**:  
Price moves outside VA and **holds** (not just wick).

**Use case:** High volatility day, directional stock.

**Entry Example:**

- Break + sustain above VAH
    
- Enter on retest of VAH
    
- Target LVN → next HVN → develop trend

## **3️⃣ LVN Imbalance Scalps**

These are momentum trades.

**Idea:**  
Price moves FAST through LVNs because liquidity is thin.

**Entry:**

- Identify LVN pocket
    
- If price enters with large volume → scalp through void
    
- Stop just outside LVN
