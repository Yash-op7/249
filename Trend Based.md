2. Identify Supply & Demand
	1. Supply zones take place in downtrend, demand zones in uptrend
	2. You want to buy from demand zones and sell from supply zones
	3. ![[Screenshot 2025-12-09 at 10.26.41 PM.png]] For example we see a large push at time t1 (left end of horizontal green rectangle), this means a lot of people want to buy at this price so if the prices comes back to this price we can make a safe assumption that people will have the same thought of buying at that price (kinda like support), similarly supply zonesm if price hits the red horizontal bar again ![[Screenshot 2025-12-09 at 10.28.15 PM.png]] 
3. In this below example since we have a new valid low, the price is in an uptrend and it will only be in a downtrend if it breaks the valid low (red line) After identifying an uptrend, we want to look for demand zone opportunities![[Pasted image 20251209223225.png]]
	we find demand zones by finding areas of consolidation -- an area where the price moved sideways before having a sharp move upwards like this![[Pasted image 20251209223339.png]]
	in this example the consolidation is right here![[Pasted image 20251209223422.png]]
	1. Find the area of consolidation before the big move
	2. Then mark the low and high of the previous candle before the big move upwards, this is our demand zone
	3. next we wait for the price to renter this demand zone and this is where we would enter setting our stop loss in this demand zone and our target at the recent highs in the uptrend, ![[Pasted image 20251209224340.png]]

So in summary this the strength of this strategy, its very accurate and we are only trading in the direction of the trend which increases likelihood of success as we are going along with the market, one final improvement to this strategy is adding one extra condition of risk to reward ratio, when using this strategy the risk to reward ratio will vary for each trade (because of how we are setting our stop loss and target according to previous supply/demand zone and previous lows/highs), for example below trade has a low risk to reward ratio of 1.7![[Pasted image 20251209231250.png]]
	the final condition is that we only want to take a trade if the risk to reward ratio is at least 2.5, so even if the other conditions are satisfied but the RR ratio is less than 2.5 we dont take the trade
